Cytogenetics

  • karyotypes
    • diploid: having two sets homologous chromosomes.
    • haploid: having one set of chromosomes.
    • hyperdiploid: having slightly more than the diploid number of chromosomes.
    • hypodiploid: having slightly fewer than the diploid number of chromosomes.
    • pseudodiploid: syn.: pseudoploid, a diploid karyotype with chromosomal translocations.
    • near-diploid
    • triploid: having or being a chromosome number three times the monoploid number.
    • near-triploid
    • tetraploid
